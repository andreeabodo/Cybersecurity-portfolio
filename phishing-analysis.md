Phishing Email Analysis

Objective
Identify signs of a phishing email and understand how attackers trick users.

Key Indicators of Phishing
- Suspicious sender email address
- Urgent language
- Fake links or mismatched URLs
- Requests for personal information 

What I Learned
Phishing attacks rely heavily on social engineering and urgency to trick users into making mistakes.

Security Takeaway
Always verify sender identity and inspect links before clicking.
